Canadian Pianist Jason Fung is a Master's of Music student at the University of Ottawa, where he is completing his final year of study under David Jalbert. After receiving a DipABRSM in Piano Performance at the age of 14, Jason’s studies then took him to Queen’s University in Kingston, where he graduated from the Bachelor’s of Music program under Ms. Dina Namer.

As winner of the Queen's University Concerto-Aria Competition, Jason returned to Kingston to perform the Grieg Concerto at the Isabel Bader Centre of Performing Arts in February, 2017.

He is also active in the Ottawa musical community as a church choir accompanist.
