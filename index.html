Born in Richmond, BC, Jason Fung spent his early years in Hong Kong, taking piano lessons from age seven. Upon his subsequent return to Canada, he began formal studies with Dina Namer of Queen’s University, graduating with a Bachelor’s of Music with Distinction in 2016. He is now studying at the University of Ottawa for a Master's in Music under David Jalbert.

Recently, Jason has performed at the Isabel Bader Centre for Performing Arts in Kingston as winner of the Queen's University Concerto Competition in 2016. He is due to make a few community performances as part of Music and Beyond in Ottawa this coming summer.
