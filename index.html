BIO Canadian-Hong Kong pianist Jason Fung is a graduate of the Université de Montréal.

Prior to completing a Master of Music (2018) with David Jalbert at the University of Ottawa, Jason studied at Queen's University, where he graduated from a B.Mus with distinction and was winner of the Queen's University Concerto-Aria Competition in 2016. As a teacher, Jason's pedagogical approach centers on building functional patterns of movement at the piano - It is his goal to provide every student a means of playing comfortably at the piano - thus freeing them up to express themselves as they wish.
