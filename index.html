Raised in Hong Kong, Canadian pianist Jason Fung is currently studying under Maneli Pirzadeh and Jimmy Brière at Université de Montrèal. Prior to completing a Master of Music (2018) with David Jalbert at the University of Ottawa, Jason also studied at Queen's University of Kingston, Ontario, where he graduated from his Bachelor's of Music (Teacher: Dina Namer) with distinction and was winner of the Queen's University Concerto-Aria Competition in 2016.

He is now active in the Ottawa-Montreal region, where he teaches and performs in solo, collaborative and church settings.
