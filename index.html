Canadian Pianist Jason Fung is a Master's of Music student at the University of Ottawa, where he is completing his final year of study under David Jalbert.

As winner of the Queen's University Concerto-Aria Competition, Jason returned to Kingston to perform the Grieg Concerto at the Isabel Bader Centre of Performing Arts in February, 2017.

He is also active in the Ottawa musical community, where he works as a church choir accompanist and recently completed engagements with Music and Beyond and Ottawa Chamberfest.

Jason is passionate about teaching students of all ages, and is comitted to helping them fulfill their musical goals at the piano. He is also available as a collaborative pianist and events pianist around the Ottawa area.
